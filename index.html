About EcoWise

EcoWise was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make sustainable living accessible and achievable for everyone. Our team of environmental scientists, developers, and sustainability experts work together to create tools that empower individuals to make a difference.

We believe that collective small actions can lead to big changes. By providing clear information, practical tools, and a supportive community, we help bridge the gap between intention and action in environmental conservation.
